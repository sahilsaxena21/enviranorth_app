# Industrial Curtain (EN921X8211)

## Overview
The Envira-North Industrial Curtain is a flexible, heavy-duty containment and separation curtain system for industrial, commercial, and agricultural spaces. Each curtain is custom-manufactured to the space — there are no fixed sizes. Typical uses are containing production heat, separating leased warehouse zones, dividing space without permanent walls, and creating manageable climate zones inside a large building.

## Best Applications
- **Production heat containment** — a Toronto manufacturer installed a curtain to contain production heat so only hot air was exhausted, significantly reducing building temperature
- **Warehouse separation** — a Montreal distribution centre used it to separate leased space
- **Flexible space division** — a Detroit manufacturer used light-penetrating curtain to divide but not permanently separate space
- **Insect and bird control** in barns and ag buildings
- **Climate zoning** inside large single-volume buildings

## Key Features & Benefits

## What Makes It Different
- **Not a fan or ventilator.** The curtain is a containment product — it partitions air, it doesn't move it. Pair with HVLS fans (Sailfin) and/or roof ventilators (Hurricane, Ecopower) for a complete climate solution.
- **Custom, not catalogued.** Every install is to-size. There is no off-the-shelf SKU — contact Envira-North for a quote based on your space.
