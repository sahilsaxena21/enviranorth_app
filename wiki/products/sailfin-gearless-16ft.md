# Altra-Air Sailfin Gearless — 16 FT (EN670X5010)

## Overview
The Sailfin Gearless 16 FT is a mid-size direct-drive HVLS ceiling fan. It uses a Nidec direct-drive motor (two moving parts, no gearbox, no oil) and ships with a built-in 20,000 lumen LED. It suits medium-to-large industrial and agricultural spaces where the 12 FT is too small but a 20 FT / 24 FT is too much for the bay width.

## What Makes It Different (vs. 16 FT Geared)
- Direct drive vs. Nord helical in-line gearmotor — no oil maintenance
- 2 HP on gearless vs. 1.5 HP on the 16 FT geared
- LED light is standard on gearless; not included on geared
- Noise: quieter than the geared variant

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- One VFD per fan.
- 230 V or 460 V motor. VFD voltage ranges: 200–240 V, 380–500 V, or 525–600 V (Schneider).
- VFD provides over-temperature and overload protection; do not use motor thermal-protection wires.
- Separate insulated ground to each VFD from the panel.
- Motor insulation class F.
- Cable-length limits and load-reactor / dV/dT-filter rules — see `guides/electrical-install-lenze.md` (Lenze) or `guides/electrical-install-schneider.md` (Schneider).

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- Minimum 10 ft (3.05 m) blade-to-floor clearance.
- Mount types: OWSJ, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam.
- Standard drops: 12", 24", 48". Extensions and custom drops available.
- Guy wires: 4 × 1/8" SS cable, 45°–60° from frame, ~90° apart, taut but not overstressed. Do NOT use turnbuckles.
- Every connection requires its own safety cable (looped at both ends).
- Minimum 1 ft (300 mm) VFD-to-fan separation.
- Only use Envira-North-supplied mounting hardware.
- Full procedure: `guides/mounting-general.md`.

## Maintenance Requirements
- No gear oil — none.
- Motor: hot-spot check + re-tighten connections at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- Blades: inspect at 6 months, then every 18–36 months.
- Mounting/guys: physical check at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- VFD panel: at 12 months, then every 18 months.
- Assumes ~5,000 hrs/year; shorter intervals in humid or aggressive environments.
- Lock out the controller for any fan/mount/guy-wire work.

## Common FAQs

**Q: How much air does the 16 FT gearless move?**
A: 127,033 CFM at full speed (80 RPM).

**Q: What HP is the 16 FT gearless?**
A: 2 HP. The equivalent geared 16 FT is 1.5 HP — the gearless is spec'd higher on HP across the range.

**Q: Can I control multiple 16 FT gearless fans from one HMI?**
A: Yes. SmartAIR Multi-Fan controls up to 10 fans on one CAT-5 / RJ45 daisy-chain. TouchAIR controls up to 20 fans. LVC controls up to 10 Schneider VFDs on one network (max 1000 ft).

**Q: Do I need a wind sensor?**
A: Mandatory in all agricultural installations. Optional (but common) in commercial outdoor-adjacent installs.

**Q: What mounting structures are supported?**
A: Open Web Steel Joist, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, and Concrete Beam. Contact Envira-North for details on each application.

**Q: Does the 16 FT gearless include an LED light?**
A: Yes — 20,000 lumen built-in LED, dimmable via SmartAIR or a 0–10 V dimmer.

**Q: What warranty applies?**
A: Lifetime on blades/hub; 3-year limited on motor and VFD; 1-year pre-approved labour. 15-year prorated service life available in US/Canada on registration within 30 days.

**Q: Is the gearless model quieter than the geared?**
A: Yes — the gearless is noted as whisper quiet due to having no gearbox.

**Q: What voids the warranty?**
A: Not following installation procedures, incorrect voltage/current, running above recommended speeds, resetting VFD parameters without Envira-North approval, using non-Envira-North hardware, skipping periodic maintenance, or any unauthorised modification.
