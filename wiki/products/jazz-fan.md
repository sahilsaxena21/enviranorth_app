# Jazz Fan

## Overview
The Jazz Fan is Envira-North's commercial / residential / institutional gearless HVLS ceiling fan. It is smaller and more compact than the Altra-Air Sailfin and is designed for open-ceiling commercial spaces like restaurants, hotels, amusement parks, gyms, and open offices. It is a single-box product that includes the mount, motor, blades, LED, HMI, and CAT-5 cable.

## Best Applications
- Open-air restaurants, amusement parks, gyms, rec centres
- Hotels, offices, light commercial spaces
- Residential high-ceiling rooms, lanais, patios (indoor use only per the installation guide — IP54)
- Any commercial space with a 10+ ft ceiling where the Sailfin's industrial look or larger diameter would be overkill
- **Not** intended for heavy industrial/agricultural applications — use Sailfin there

## Key Features & Benefits
- Gearless direct-drive brushless DC motor — compact and efficient
- Smart voltage: auto-detects 120–240 VAC, 50/60 Hz — no jumper changes
- Integrated 1,980-lumen dimmable LED (replaceable kit, EN561X3675) with 20 brightness levels, separately controlled from fan speed
- Single-box packaging — mount, motor, blades, LED, HMI, and CAT-5 cable all in one
- Plug-and-play install with pre-wired connections
- On-board HMI control (1 fan) or Jazz Multi-Fan HMI (up to 5 fans)
- UL / CSA / CE certified
- Thermal limiting: the controller automatically reduces fan speed if the motor or drive gets too hot — no user action needed
- Modbus 485 signal built in for connection to building automation and fire suppression systems
- Universal Mounting System supports sloped ceilings from 0–18.5° with 360° swivel
- Blade design uses varying angle of attack (6° at tip to 14° maximum) and serrated leading edge to minimize noise and evenly distribute airflow
- Blade span: 58.5" tip-to-tip; zinc-coated steel mounting plate with injection-moulded fibre-filled polypropylene

## What Makes It Different
- **Own network.** Jazz fans cannot be mixed with standard Sailfin controls on the same network. Jazz has its own Jazz HMI and Jazz Multi-Fan HMI. TouchAIR can talk to a Jazz fan network but must treat it as a separate zone.
- **Smaller scale.** 7–10 ft diameters vs. 8–24 ft for Sailfin. 45 MPH–6 MPH breeze range; 45,000 CFM max vs. 315,026 CFM max for the 24 FT Sailfin.
- **Indoor only.** IP54 / NEMA 3. Not rated for washdown or outdoor exposure.
- **Included everything.** Ships as a single box with all hardware, mount, HMI, and cable.
- **Gearless motor with hidden wiring.** The hollow shaft runs through the motor assembly to hide power and control wires, giving a clean finished look without sacrificing durability.
- **Colour & finish options.** Six standard colours (Alabaster, Cobalt, Onyx, Radiance, Slate, Sterling) plus custom colours and textured finishes (Carbon Fibre, Leather, Mahogany, Barn Board).

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- **Input:** 120–240 VAC, 50–60 Hz. The fan auto-detects voltage.
- **Current draw:** 4.6 A at 120 V, 2.5 A at 240 V.
- **Fusing:** 10 A fast-blow for 110–120 VAC; 5 A fast-blow for 220–240 VAC.
- **Power cable:** min 16 AWG 3-conductor, rated 300 VAC. Copper only. Permanent connection only.
- **Control cable:** max 30 m unshielded.
- **Supply earthing:** TN-S. Touch current of protective earth conductor can reach up to 9 mA AC.
- **Do not lift or hang the fan by the power cable** — the safety disconnect may trip power to the fan.
- Only qualified electricians may install the drive and motor connections. After disconnecting input power, wait 5 minutes for capacitors to discharge before working on the drive or cable.

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- **Clearances:**
  - Min 12" (304.8 mm) from blade leading edge to obstruction above or below
  - Min 8" (203 mm) from side of fan to any obstruction
  - Min 120" (3,050 mm) from floor to fan leading edge
- **Mounting kits included for:** glu-lam, purlin, OWSJ, wood truss. Each uses the EN562X1152 mounting brackets and EN562X2510 studded mount assembly.
- **Mounting plate:** EN562X1100. Brackets fasten to the structural member with ¼" × 1¼" hex cap screws and nylocks per the kit.
- **Slope:** Universal Mounting System supports 0–18.5° sloped ceilings with a full 360° swivel. Set screw on the ball joint/swivel is tightened once the fan is level.
- **Custom mount lengths** available on request.
- **Safety cable:** 3/32" (EN022X2222) braided stainless steel cable is fastened to the structure and runs the length of the assembly during installation. Cable clamps: EN823X6955 (1/8"). Allows ¾" (19 mm) of slack.
- **Motor assembly fasteners:** Four ¼" studs with nylon-inserted lock nuts attach the fan assembly to the mounting hardware. Four M5 fasteners bolt the motor cap to the hub. Four 1¼" × ¼" hex head bolts with serrated washers lock each blade.
- **Power cable:** must be fastened to structure with no slack — required for the safety disconnect to function correctly.
- **Do NOT lift, carry, or hang fan by power cable.**
- **Do NOT substitute blades.** Use only the blades marked for this fan assembly.
- **Do NOT use non-Envira-North mounting hardware.**
- Every installer must use a safety harness.
- Consult a structural engineer before installation.
- All installation and electrical work must follow the National Electrical Code (CSA C22.1 / ANSI NFPA 70) and local codes.

### LF/SP Assembly (EN561X3660)
- **Europe (CE): Required** — must be installed as directed for EMC compliance.
- **North America (UL/CSA): Strongly recommended** — the Jazz Fan (EN575X0010) does not have a built-in surge suppression device; a Jazz LF/SP Assembly or other surge suppression device (minimum: whole-panel surge protector) is highly recommended on the fan's input power line.

### Fire Shutdown Connection (Optional)
Connect the supplied connector to a relay contact in a fire detection / suppression system enclosure to automatically shut the fan down on alarm.

## Maintenance Requirements
- **Gearless** — no gearbox, no oil.
- **Thermal limiting is automatic** — the controller reduces fan speed if the motor or drive gets too hot, then resumes. No user intervention.
- Lock out the controller during any fan or mount maintenance.
- Never do electrical work with input power applied; wait 5 minutes after disconnect for capacitors to discharge.
- No maintenance within a 6 m (20 ft) horizontal radius of the fan or 1.2 m (4 ft) below blade level while in operation.
- Safety barrier at 6 m radius during maintenance.
- Risk assessment and tailboard meeting required before any maintenance work.

### Recommended Maintenance Schedule
**Initial six months:**
- Check for hot spots
- Re-tighten all loose electrical connections
- Check all nuts / bolts / clamps (missing, loose, or damaged)
- Physical check of safety cable; re-tighten clamps if required

**Repeat every eighteen months thereafter** (same checks as above)

## Common FAQs

**Q: What sizes are available?**
A: 7 ft, 8 ft, 9 ft, and 10 ft (2.1–3.0 m).

**Q: Can I put Jazz fans on the same SmartAIR or LVC network as my Sailfin fans?**
A: No. Jazz fans must be on their own separate network. The only HMI option that can span both is TouchAIR, and even then the Jazz fans are a separate zone.

**Q: Is the Jazz Fan rated for outdoor use?**
A: No. IP54 / NEMA 3, and the installation guide explicitly says "For indoor use only." For covered outdoor (lanai, open-wall restaurant) installations, confirm with Envira-North Customer Support.

**Q: What's the LED light output?**
A: 1,980 lumens, 20 W, dimmable. This is an integrated LED light kit (EN561X3675).

**Q: Do I need a separate VFD for each Jazz Fan?**
A: No — unlike the Sailfin, the Jazz Fan has on-board controls. One Jazz HMI controls one Jazz fan (or a Jazz Multi-Fan HMI controls up to 5 fans).

**Q: What voltage does it need?**
A: 120–240 VAC at 50–60 Hz. The fan auto-detects voltage within that range — no jumpers to set.

**Q: What's the recommended fuse?**
A: 10 A fast-blow for 110–120 VAC, 5 A fast-blow for 220–240 VAC.

**Q: Can I dim the LED?**
A: Yes — the integrated LED is dimmable from the Jazz HMI.

**Q: How many Jazz fans can I control from one HMI?**
A: 1 from a single-fan Jazz HMI, or up to 5 from a Jazz Multi-Fan HMI. For more than 5 Jazz fans, TouchAIR can supervise multiple Jazz zones.

**Q: What mount types are supported?**
A: The included hardware covers glu-lam, purlin, OWSJ, and wood truss mounts. Other mount types are not in the included kit — contact Envira-North.

**Q: What maintenance does it need?**
A: Gearless, so no oil. The controller does thermal protection automatically. Follow the maintenance schedule in the installation guide, lock out during any physical work, and wait 5 minutes after power disconnect before touching the drive.

**Q: Who can install the electrical side?**
A: Only qualified electricians, per the installation guide's warning.
