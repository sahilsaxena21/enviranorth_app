# Altra-Air Sailfin Gearless — 24 FT (EN670X5016)

## Overview
The Sailfin Gearless 24 FT is the largest Altra-Air Sailfin. Direct-drive Nidec motor, two moving parts, built-in 20,000 lumen LED, and the highest CFM in the lineup. The Design & Engineering case studies reference 24 FT Sailfins in dairy barns (8 fans keeping 200 cows cool, milk production steady) and in a 50,000 sq ft New York manufacturing facility that used a single 24 FT fan to de-stratify production heat and eliminate separate heaters.

## What Makes It Different (vs. 24 FT Geared)
- Zero oil maintenance (no gearbox)
- LED light included on gearless; not on geared
- Quieter (whisper quiet) than geared variant
- Same 2 HP rating, same CFM (315,026), same RPM (53), same weight (157 kg / 347 lbs) — the differences are drive, maintenance, noise, and the LED

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- One VFD per fan. 230 V or 460 V.
- VFD handles over-temperature and overload protection; motor thermal wires not used.
- Separate insulated ground to each VFD.
- Motor insulation class F.
- Cable-length limits and filter rules: `guides/electrical-install-lenze.md` (Lenze) or `guides/electrical-install-schneider.md` (Schneider).

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- 10 ft minimum blade-to-floor clearance.
- Mount types: OWSJ, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam.
- Standard drops: 12", 24", 48"; custom available.
- 4-point 1/8" SS guy wires at 45°–60°, ~90° apart. No turnbuckles.
- Every connection gets its own safety cable.
- Min 1 ft (300 mm) VFD-to-fan separation.

## Maintenance Requirements
- No gear oil.
- Motor hot-spot check + re-tighten at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- Blade inspection at 6 months, then 18–36 months.
- Mount/guy check at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- VFD panel at 12 months, then every 18 months.
- Lock out controller during all fan/mount work.

## Common FAQs

**Q: How much air does a 24 FT gearless move?**
A: 315,026 CFM at 53 RPM — the most of any Sailfin.

**Q: How many 24 FT fans cool 200 cows?**
A: A Canadian dairy case study in the Design & Engineering brochure uses eight 24 FT Sailfins for 200 cows. Specific cooling capacity depends on barn geometry and climate — contact Envira-North for a site-specific layout.

**Q: Can one 24 FT Sailfin replace air conditioning?**
A: It depends on the application. In the Dubai case study, a single 24 FT Sailfin raised the A/C set point 2 °C and saved 34 % on cooling cost — it supplemented, not replaced, A/C. In a New York manufacturer, one 24 FT Sailfin de-stratified production heat and eliminated the need for heaters. For your specific space, contact Envira-North.

**Q: Why does the geared 24 FT have the same CFM as the gearless 24 FT?**
A: The spec sheets list the same 315,026 CFM / 53 RPM / 157 kg for both the geared and gearless 24 FT. The differences are drive type (gearmotor vs. direct drive), maintenance (oil vs. none), LED (not included vs. included), and noise (louder vs. whisper quiet).

**Q: What voltage does the 24 FT gearless accept?**
A: 230 V or 460 V. The Schneider VFD paired with it is available in 200–240 V, 380–500 V, or 525–600 V ranges and accepts single-phase input.

**Q: What's the minimum fan speed?**
A: 15 Hz / 27 %. Running below this will overheat the motor and void the warranty.

**Q: Do I need a wind sensor?**
A: Yes, if this is an agricultural install. Optional for other installations but recommended if the barn/warehouse has large outdoor openings.

**Q: Can I control it from my building automation system?**
A: Yes. The recommended approach for BAS integration is TouchAIR (NEMA4X, up to 20 fans) paired with Schneider VFDs or ModAIR. Consult `controls/touchair.md` and contact Envira-North for BAS wiring specifics.
