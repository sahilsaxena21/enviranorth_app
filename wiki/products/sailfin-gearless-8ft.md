# Altra-Air Sailfin Gearless — 8 FT (EN670X5002)

## Overview
The Sailfin Gearless 8 FT is the smallest size in the direct-drive Altra-Air Sailfin Gearless lineup. Like all gearless Sailfins, it uses a Nidec direct-drive motor with only two moving parts, no gear oil, and a built-in 20,000 lumen LED light standard. At 59,494 CFM it is the lowest-output Sailfin, suited to smaller bays, retail spaces, and facilities where a large fan would be oversized.

## What Makes It Different (vs. 8 FT Geared)
- **Maintenance:** no gear-oil check or oil change required.
- **LED light:** the Gearless 8 FT ships with a 20,000 lumen LED; the geared 8 FT does not include an LED.
- **Noise:** gearless is 45 dBA whisper quiet vs. 62–63 dBA for geared Sailfins.
- **Drive:** direct Nidec motor vs. Nord gearmotor on the geared.
- **Pre-start:** the geared version ships with a gearbox vent plug that MUST be removed before first start-up. The gearless has no such step.

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- Every Sailfin Gearless 8 FT requires its own VFD (one per fan).
- Available voltages: 230 V or 460 V. Schneider VFD ranges cover 200–240 V, 380–500 V, and 525–600 V.
- The VFD provides over-temperature and overload protection — thermal-protection wires in the motor junction box are **not** to be connected unless directed by Envira-North.
- A separate insulated ground must be run from the electrical panel to each VFD.
- Motor insulation class F. Wiring must conform to the National Electrical Code and local codes.
- For Lenze VFD cable-length limits and load-reactor / dV/dT-filter rules, see `guides/electrical-install-lenze.md`.
- For Schneider VFD / SmartAIR wiring, see `guides/electrical-install-schneider.md`.

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- Minimum blade-to-floor clearance: 3.05 m (10 ft). The fan has an unguarded impeller — do not install anywhere readily accessible to people or animals.
- Supported mount types: OWSJ, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam.
- Standard drop lengths: 12", 24", 48". Custom lengths available. Extensions (24" or 48") can be added.
- Minimum 1 ft (300 mm) separation between the VFD and the fan motor.
- 4-point 1/8" stainless steel guy wire system; guys at 45°–60° from fan frame to structure, ~90° apart from each other. Do NOT use turnbuckles.
- Every connection between components (mount, extension, fan frame) requires its own safety cable.
- Only use mounting hardware supplied by Envira-North.
- Full procedure: `guides/mounting-general.md`.

## Maintenance Requirements
(See also `guides/maintenance-schedule.md`.)
- No gear oil maintenance — this variant has none.
- **Motor:** check for hot spots and re-tighten electrical connections at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- **Blades:** inspect for damage, levelness, and clean as needed at 6 months, then every 18–36 months.
- **Mounting / guy wires:** physical check at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- **VFD control panel:** check at 12 months, then every 18 months.
- Schedule assumes ~5,000 hrs/year. In high humidity or aggressive environments, shorten intervals.
- Never perform maintenance while the fan is powered. Lock out the controller during any fan/mount/guy-wire work.

## Common FAQs

**Q: Is the 8 FT the smallest gearless Sailfin?**
A: Yes. The Gearless lineup spans 8 FT to 24 FT. The 8 FT Gearless (EN670X5002) is the smallest. For the geared lineup, the 8 FT geared (EN675X5002) is also available.

**Q: Does the 8 FT gearless come with an LED light?**
A: Yes. A 20,000 lumen dimmable LED is standard on all gearless Sailfins, including the 8 FT.

**Q: Do I need a VFD?**
A: Yes. Every Altra-Air fan, including the 8 FT gearless, requires one VFD per fan. The VFD provides soft-start, speed control, and overload protection.

**Q: Can I use a 120 V wall switch to dim the LED?**
A: No. A standard 120 VAC switch only gives on/off at full brightness. For dimming without SmartAIR you need a 0–10 V LED-compatible dimmer such as a Leviton DS710-10Z or a Lutron DVSTV-WH.

**Q: What's the minimum ceiling height?**
A: 10 ft (3.05 m) from blade leading edge to floor. The blades are unguarded.

**Q: Is gear-oil maintenance required?**
A: No — the gearless variant has no gearbox and no oil.

**Q: What warranty does it carry?**
A: Lifetime on blades, hub, and mounting system; 3-year limited on motor and VFD control panel; 1-year labour (pre-approved). A 15-year prorated service-life warranty is available in the US and Canada if you register within 30 days of purchase. The 15-year warranty is not transferable to a new owner.

**Q: Is a wind sensor required?**
A: Mandatory in all agricultural installations. Optional but common for outdoor-exposed commercial installs.

**Q: What is the effective coverage area?**
A: Maximum effective diameter of 18 m (60 ft) — defined as where horizontal airspeed at 1.0 ft above floor drops below 0.2 m/s in an empty room.
