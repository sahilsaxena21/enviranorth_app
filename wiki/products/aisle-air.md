# Aisle-Air Fan (EN921X8235)

## Overview
The Aisle-Air is a high-velocity, high-speed axial fan designed to push a focused stream of air down long, narrow spaces like warehouse rack aisles or livestock aisles. It is not an HVLS fan — it is a high-velocity spot-cooling fan with a fibreglass housing and minimal footprint. It mounts on a post, rack, or ceiling and can cover aisles up to 80 ft long with up to 15 MPH air speeds.

## Best Applications
- Warehouse rack aisles — keeps long aisles cool, keeps floors dry, controls insects
- Livestock aisles and barn alleys where focused airflow is preferred over large-diameter HVLS
- Spot cooling where structural space is tight (rack-mounted use)
- Eliminating temperature differentials in tight spaces

## Key Features & Benefits
- 15 MPH focused air stream — much higher velocity than an HVLS fan
- Small physical footprint — fits in tight spots
- Fibreglass housing for corrosion resistance
- Integrated IP55 controls
- Modbus communication
- Brushless DC motor — maintenance-free
- Optional OSHA-compliant guard and velocity booster
- Quick installation from rack, post, or ceiling
- Multiple pre-set mounting angles (rotate and secure via two 3/8" × 1½" bolts before mounting)

## What Makes It Different
Unlike the Sailfin and Jazz fans, which are HVLS ceiling fans, the Aisle-Air is a high-velocity point-of-use fan. It does not circulate a whole room — it pushes a concentrated stream down an aisle. Pick it when you need directional cooling over a long narrow space, not broad ceiling-level circulation.

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- Input voltage: 200–240 VAC or 380–480 VAC 3-phase
- Current: ~2 A at 200–240 V
- Integrated controls (no separate VFD needed — ECblue BASIC-MODBUS motor controller is built in)
- Wiring must conform to the National Electrical Code and local codes
- **Power cable:** 4 × 16 AWG
- **Control (Modbus) cable:** 10 ft Cat5 cable (included with fan)
- **Wiring connections (ECblue BASIC-MODBUS):**
  - 1L (Black), 2L (Black), 3L (Black) — 3-phase line inputs
  - EP (Green/Yellow) — earth/protective ground
  - VDC 24+ (White/Brown) — 24 V DC supply
  - Ground (Brown)
  - (+D) A (Blue) — Modbus A
  - (−D) B (White/Blue) — Modbus B

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- **Supported mount types:** OWSJ, Post, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam
- **Standard drop lengths:** 12", 24", 48" (wood beam mounting does not use the standard mount package)
- **Mount assembly:** EN400X3420 Aisle-Air Fans Mount Assembly. Has several angle positions — rotate to desired angle and fasten with two 3/8" × 1½" bolts **before** mounting the fan.
- **Side mount plates** come pre-installed on both sides of the Aisle-Air unit. Attach the mount assembly using EN823X6002 (½" × 1½" bolt), EN823X6901 (½" washers ×2), and EN823X6301 (½" nylock) in the lower hole on each bracket; EN823X6008 (3/8" × 1½" bolt), EN823X6381 (3/8" washers ×2), and EN823X6999 (3/8" nylock) in the upper holes.
- Once the angle is selected, tighten the EN823X6999 (3/8" nylock) to lock the pivot in place.
- Install the selected 12"/24"/48" mount to the EN400X3420 using the remaining EN823X6002 bolts (×4), EN823X6901 washers (×8), and EN823X6301 nylocks (×4).
- **Safety cable:** wrap EN823X6950 3/16" (4 ft) safety cable around the EN400X3420 mount assembly and the mount. Secure using EN823X6952 cable clamps 3/16" (×4) by looping the ends.
- **Orientation:** check direction of airflow before installing — the fan must be oriented correctly for the aisle.
- **Cable clamp technique:** U-bolt on the short turn-back side; saddle on the live end.
- **Extensions:** 24" or 48" extensions available. Every connection between components (mounts, extensions, fan frame) must include its own safety cable.
- Safety harness required for all personnel during install.

## Maintenance Requirements
- No maintenance on the fan or mount while powered or in operation.
- No maintenance on the fan controller while powered (unless the task requires it, e.g., programming/troubleshooting).
- No work within 6 m horizontal radius of the fan, or 4 ft below the fan, while it is operating.
- Safety barrier at 6 m radius during maintenance.
- Controller must be locked out during fan/mount/guy-wire maintenance.
- All personnel must wear appropriate PPE and perform a risk assessment before work.
- A tailboard meeting and checklist (with emergency contacts) must be completed before any work begins.
- **Brushless DC motor is maintenance-free** (per the brochure) — the scheduled tasks focus on mount, guys, fasteners, and drop integrity.

### Recommended Maintenance Schedule
**Initial six months:**
- Physical check of fan guy wires; tighten clamps if required
- Check all nuts / bolts / clamps (missing, loose, or damaged)
- Physical check of safety cable; tighten clamps if required

**After every eighteen months thereafter** (same checks as above)

## Common FAQs

**Q: How far can the Aisle-Air push air?**
A: Up to 80 ft down an aisle at up to 15 MPH.

**Q: Is the Aisle-Air an HVLS fan?**
A: No. It is a high-velocity, focused-airflow axial fan. HVLS is a different category (Sailfin, Alite 3, Jazz).

**Q: What voltages does it accept?**
A: 200–240 VAC or 380–480 VAC 3-phase.

**Q: Does it need a VFD?**
A: No. The Aisle-Air has integrated ECBasic-Modbus motor control. It talks Modbus directly.

**Q: Can I mount it on a rack?**
A: Yes — post mount and ceiling mount are both supported, and the brochure specifically lists "mounted directly on the racking" as a use case.

**Q: What mounting types are supported?**
A: OWSJ, post mount, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, and Concrete Beam. The standard mount package covers all except wood beam.

**Q: Does it have a guard?**
A: Optionally — an OSHA-compliant guard is available.

**Q: Is the motor maintenance-free?**
A: Yes — brushless DC. The brochure says "maintenance-free."

**Q: Does it need guy wires?**
A: The installation guide's maintenance section references guy wires, suggesting extended or high drops may use them. For the basic post or ceiling mount with a 12–48" drop, guy wires are not typical. Confirm guy-wire requirements for your specific drop length with Envira-North.

**Q: What's the IP rating?**
A: IP55 — dust-protected and protected against low-pressure water jets from any direction.

**Q: What's the recommended warranty?**
A: 3-year limited warranty.
