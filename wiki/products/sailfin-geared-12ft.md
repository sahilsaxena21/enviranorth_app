# Altra-Air Sailfin Geared — 12 FT (EN675X5006)

## Overview
The Sailfin Geared 12 FT is a small-to-mid size HVLS ceiling fan driven by a Nord helical in-line gearmotor. It shares the 5-blade Sailfin airfoil design with the rest of the range and is intended for smaller industrial bays, workshops, and barns. A gearless 12 FT variant (EN670X5006) is also available if you want to avoid gear-oil maintenance and want a built-in LED light.

## Best Applications
- Small warehouses and light manufacturing
- Small to mid-size barns
- Workshops and service bays
- Any application where the gearless 12 FT would be over-spec'd or where the broader voltage compatibility (400/575 V) is needed

## Key Features & Benefits
- Nord helical in-line gearmotor (2.5× stronger blade design per AMCA testing)
- 5-blade Sailfin airfoil with up to 23° angle of attack; blade top width 10¼" for maximum airflow
- 200°C ISR motor wire (VFD duty)
- Broad voltage compatibility (4 options) — useful where the gearless's 230/460 V range isn't enough
- Lifetime warranty on blades and hub
- Creates an evaporative cooling effect of 3 to 4°C throughout the facility
- UL, CSA, and CE certified (confirmed in the 2018 Dairy Catalogue)
- Non-disruptive airflow; whisper quiet operation
- No drive belts or belt tighteners to maintain

## What Makes It Different (vs. 12 FT Gearless)
- 1 HP (geared) vs. 2 HP (gearless)
- 50.4 kg / 111 lbs (geared) vs. 111 kg / 245 lbs (gearless) — the geared 12 FT is significantly lighter
- Voltage options: 230/400/460/575 V (geared) vs. 230/460 V (gearless)
- No LED light (gearless includes 20,000 lumen built-in LED)
- Oil maintenance required (check at 18 months, change every 20,000 hrs)
- Louder than the gearless variant

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- One VFD per fan. 230/400/460/575 V options.
- VFD over-temperature and overload protection — do not connect motor thermal wires.
- Separate insulated ground per VFD.
- Motor insulation class F.
- Cable-length rules: see `guides/electrical-install-lenze.md`.

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- 10 ft minimum blade-to-floor clearance.
- Mount types: OWSJ, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam.
- Standard drops 12"/24"/48"; custom available.
- Guy wires: 4 × 1/8" SS, 45°–60°, ~90° apart, no turnbuckles.
- Every connection gets a safety cable.
- Min 1 ft VFD-to-fan separation.
- **Pre-start:** remove the rubber vent plug from the gearbox; discard the pink tag if attached.

## Maintenance Requirements
- **Gear oil:** check at 18 months, change every 20,000 hrs of operation.
- Motor: 6 months initial, then every 18 months.
- Blades: 6 months initial, then every 18–36 months.
- Mount/guys: 6 months initial, then every 18 months.
- VFD panel: 12 months initial, then every 18 months.
- Schedule assumes ~5,000 hrs/year.

## Compatible Accessories & Controls
Same as other geared Sailfins: EssentialAIR, ZoneAIR, CommandAIR, ModAIR (Lenze-based), or Schneider VFD + SmartAIR / TouchAIR. LVC + TFD-1 analogue control. Wind Sensor (mandatory agricultural). Fire relay optional.

## Common FAQs

**Q: What's the difference between the 12 FT geared and 12 FT gearless?**
A: Drive (gearmotor vs. direct drive), HP (1 vs. 2), weight (50.4 kg vs. 111 kg), voltage options (4 vs. 2), LED (not included vs. 20,000 lumen standard), and oil maintenance (yes vs. none). Both move 70,424 CFM at 105 RPM.

**Q: Why is the geared 12 FT so much lighter than the gearless?**
A: That is what the 2022 spec sheets list (50.4 kg geared vs. 111 kg gearless). If this affects your mounting calculation, verify with Envira-North Customer Support before installation.

**Q: Does the 12 FT geared need an oil change right away?**
A: No. Check the oil level at 18 months. Change the oil every 20,000 hours of normal operation.

**Q: Can I use a SmartAIR touchscreen with this fan?**
A: Yes — SmartAIR works via the Schneider VFD, which is compatible with the geared Sailfin. ModAIR (Lenze + ModBus) is the other path for SmartAIR/TouchAIR integration.

**Q: What's the minimum speed I can program?**
A: 15 Hz / 27 %. Below this the motor will overheat and the warranty will be voided.

**Q: What voltage is supported?**
A: 230, 400, 460, or 575 V. Single-phase is supported on 230 V via the Schneider VFD (use L1-L2 + PE).

## Blade Aerodynamic Advantage
- Stall angles as high as 23°
- Always stalls gradually — eliminating tip stalling (the primary cause of blade noise and damaging vibration)
- Eliminates span-wise pumping — the primary cause of efficiency loss in all rotating systems
- Hyper-stability lowers noise and vibrations that cause wear on blades and drive train

## Clearance Requirements (Dairy Catalogue)
- Min 60" (152 cm) centre of fan to roof deck for ideal operating performance
- Min 30" (76 cm) from fan blade's leading edge to any obstruction above or below fan
- Min 18" (46 cm) from side of fan to any obstruction
- Min 144" (366 cm) floor to fan leading edge height
- Ideal height of 180" (457 cm) from floor to blades (eliminates safety issues with equipment)

## Dairy Applications
- Fans range from 12 ft to 24 ft diameter — the 12 FT is the smallest dairy-catalogue size
- Capable of moving nearly 315,000 CFM (148,000 L/S) across the range
- Higher average wind speeds than traditional cooling fans
- Reduce cow crowding; increase milk production; reduce heat stress
- Minimizes the impact of heat on cow fertility
- Automatic controls allow the fan to only operate when necessary — reducing energy costs
- Fans are ceiling-mounted out of the way of moving equipment
- Electrical controls can be located in one central lockable location
- Moving air reduces birds and flies in the facility
- Less wiring and labour required vs. traditional cooling fans

## Agricultural / Hog Barn Applications
- Provides constant mixing of air within the room
- Inlets counter-balanced and located directly above the fan
- Fans distribute fresh air throughout the room — leading to a 30% reduction in exhaust capacity required
- More air speed reduces fly issues
- Fewer fans = lower operational cost and less maintenance
- Reverse capability enables winter circulation without drafts
