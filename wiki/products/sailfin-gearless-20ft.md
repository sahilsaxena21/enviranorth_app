# Altra-Air Sailfin Gearless — 20 FT (EN670X5014)

## Overview
The Sailfin Gearless 20 FT is a large direct-drive HVLS ceiling fan. It is one of Envira-North's most common sizes for large industrial bays and is cited in the Design & Engineering documents for rock-climbing facilities, Toronto warehouse retrofits, and dairy barns. Direct drive means two moving parts, no gearbox, and a 20,000 lumen built-in LED.

## Best Applications
- 20,000+ sq ft industrial bays (a single 20 FT fan was used to cool a Toronto rock-climbing facility and reduce heating costs ~10%)
- Large dairy barns (example: five 20 FT Sailfins in a 100,000 sq ft Toronto facility for de-stratification)
- High-ceiling manufacturing and warehouse operations
- Agricultural installations — wind sensor is mandatory

## Key Features & Benefits
- Gearless direct drive — zero oil maintenance
- Built-in 20,000 lumen LED (standard)
- 2 HP Nidec motor with 200°C ISR VFD-duty winding
- 5-blade Sailfin airfoil with up to 23° angle of attack

- Maximum 5.0 A draw
- 264 CFM/Watt efficiency
- Minimal 2.5" blade flex

## What Makes It Different (vs. 20 FT Geared)
- Same SKU suffix numbers (5014) but different product line: EN670X5014 (gearless) vs. EN675X5014 (geared)
- No gearbox, no gear oil, no oil-change schedule
- LED light standard on gearless; not included on geared
- Quieter operation (whisper quiet vs. 62–63 dB geared)
- Same 2 HP on both 20 FT variants

## Power & Electrical Requirements
- One VFD per fan.
- 230 V or 460 V.
- VFD provides overtemperature/overload protection; do not use motor thermal wires.
- Separate insulated ground to each VFD from the panel.
- Motor insulation class F.
- Cable-length limits and load reactor / dV/dT rules: see `guides/electrical-install-lenze.md` (Lenze) or `guides/electrical-install-schneider.md` (Schneider).

## Mounting & Installation Notes
- 10 ft minimum blade-to-floor clearance.
- Mount types: OWSJ, Purlin Z, I-Beam, Wood Beam, Concrete Beam.
- Standard drops: 12", 24", 48". Custom and extensions available.
- 4-point guy wires at 45°–60°, ~90° apart. Do NOT use turnbuckles.
- Every connection requires its own safety cable.
- Min 1 ft (300 mm) VFD-to-fan separation.
- Only Envira-North-supplied mounting hardware allowed.

## Maintenance Requirements
- No gear oil.
- Motor: hot-spot check + connection re-tighten at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- Blades: inspect at 6 months, then every 18–36 months.
- Mounting/guys: physical check at 6 months, then every 18 months.
- VFD panel: at 12 months, then every 18 months.
- Lock out controller during any fan/mount work.

## Common FAQs

**Q: How many 20 FT gearless fans can I control with one SmartAIR Multi-Fan?**
A: Up to 10 fans on one SmartAIR Multi-Fan HMI via an RJ45 / CAT-5 daisy-chain from a control box. For 11+ fans, use TouchAIR (up to 20).

**Q: What CFM does the 20 FT gearless produce?**
A: 176,200 CFM at 63 RPM full speed.

**Q: What HP is the motor?**
A: 2 HP. This matches the 2 HP recommendation Envira-North gives for Schneider VFD pairing with any Sailfin size.

**Q: Can I mount this on a wood beam?**
A: Yes — wood beam is one of the supported mount types. Contact Envira-North for application-specific mounting instructions.

**Q: Is the 20 FT gearless the same weight as the 20 FT geared?**
A: Yes — both weigh 140 kg (320 lbs) per the spec sheets.

**Q: Does the gearless model need a wind sensor?**
A: Mandatory in agricultural applications. Optional for other installations but recommended if the space has outdoor air exposure. If no wind sensor is installed and the system is SmartAIR, a jumper must be installed in its place or the fan will not operate.

**Q: Can I run the fan below 15 Hz?**
A: No. 15 Hz / 27 % is the minimum fan speed. Programming below this will overheat the motor at low speed and will void the warranty.

**Q: What's the difference between EN670X5014 and EN675X5014?**
A: Both are 20 FT Sailfins. EN670X5014 is the gearless direct-drive variant with the LED. EN675X5014 is the geared variant with a Nord gearmotor and no LED.
