# Alite 3 HVLS Fan (EN921X8230)

## Overview
The Alite 3 is Envira-North's lower-cost HVLS ceiling fan. It is a simpler, lighter-weight alternative to the Altra-Air Sailfin for facilities that want HVLS circulation without the Sailfin's higher price point and feature set. It uses a 0.75 kW (1 HP) gear motor and a 3-blade design. Available in two sizes: 4.7 m (15 ft) and 7.1 m (23 ft).

## Best Applications
- Facilities that want HVLS circulation on a tight budget
- Low-to-medium duty warehouses and workshops
- Dairy barns and livestock spaces where the Sailfin's full feature set is not required
- Applications where minimising weight matters (the 3-blade design is lighter than the 5-blade Sailfin)

## Key Features & Benefits
- Cost-effective alternative to the Altra-Air Sailfin
- 0.75 kW (1 HP) gear motor — nominal power consumption
- 3-blade design minimises weight
- Tested to AMCA standards
- Quiet performance: 57–62 dBA
- Impressive efficiency: up to 288 CFM/Watt on the 23 ft model (catalogue: 285 CFM/Watt)
- Air speed: up to 4.5 MPH breeze (per 2025 catalogue)
- Blade angle of attack: 18° (per 2025 catalogue)
- High-impact polystyrene leading edge on blades
- Minimal maintenance (per the brochure)
- Both 50 Hz and 60 Hz compatible

## What Makes It Different
- **3 blades vs. 5 on the Sailfin.** Lighter, cheaper to manufacture, but less airflow per rotation.
- **Lower HP.** 1 HP fixed across both sizes vs. 1–2 HP on the Sailfin range.
- **No LED light option** — Alite 3 does not include or offer an LED light kit in the source documents.
- **Smaller control option set.** The Alite 3 source brochure does not call out SmartAIR, TouchAIR, or ModAIR compatibility specifically — it is typically paired with simpler LVC-based controls. Confirm specific control compatibility with Envira-North.
- **15 ft and 23 ft** are the only sizes, which don't map directly to Sailfin sizes.

## Common FAQs

**Q: What sizes is the Alite 3 available in?**
A: 4.7 m (15 ft) and 7.1 m (23 ft).

**Q: How efficient is the Alite 3?**
A: 148 CFM/Watt on the 15 ft model, 288 CFM/Watt on the 23 ft model. The brochure positions this as a cost-effective alternative to the Sailfin.

**Q: How many blades does the Alite 3 have?**
A: 3 blades. This is different from the 5-blade Sailfin and Jazz fans.

**Q: What HP is the motor?**
A: 0.75 kW, which is 1 HP. Same motor on both the 15 ft and 23 ft versions.

**Q: How noisy is the Alite 3?**
A: 62 dBA at the 15 ft size; 57 dBA at the 23 ft size.

**Q: What voltage does it need?**
A: 230 V or 460 V. The motor runs on 50 Hz or 60 Hz.

**Q: Is it cheaper than the Sailfin?**
A: The Alite 3 is positioned in the brochure as a "cost-effective alternative" to traditional HVLS fans. Pricing is not documented in the wiki — contact Envira-North for current pricing.

**Q: Does the Alite 3 have an LED light?**
A: No — not in the source documents.

**Q: What control do I use with the Alite 3?**
