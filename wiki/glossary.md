# Glossary

**AMCA** — Air Movement and Control Association. Industry standards body; several Envira-North performance numbers are tested to AMCA standards.

**BAS** — Building Automation System. TouchAIR is designed to integrate with BAS for centralised fan control.

**CAT-5** — Category 5 Ethernet cable. Used by SmartAIR, TouchAIR, Jazz HMI, and CommandAIR for HMI-to-VFD communication.

**CFM** — Cubic Feet per Minute. Unit of air volume flow rate.

**CFM/Watt** — a measure of fan energy efficiency (higher is better). Used in Alite 3 and Sailfin marketing comparisons.

**dV/dT filter** — output-side filter for a VFD that smooths the voltage rise-time seen by the motor. Required on Lenze VFD-to-motor runs over 400 ft (230 V), over 250 ft (460 V), or over 160 ft (600 V). Using one requires upsizing the VFD by +1 HP.

**ECM** — Electronically Commutated Motor. Used in Direct-Air destratification fans and referenced in Ecopower.

**HMI** — Human-Machine Interface (typically a touchscreen). SmartAIR, TouchAIR, Jazz HMI, and Jazz Multi-Fan HMI are all HMIs.

**HVLS** — High Volume, Low Speed. The fan category for Sailfin, Alite 3, and Jazz fans. HVLS fans move a lot of air at low RPM for quiet, efficient circulation.

**IP54 / IP55 / IP66** — Ingress Protection ratings. IP54 = dust-protected + splash-resistant (Jazz enclosure). IP55 = dust-protected + low-pressure water jet (Aisle-Air). IP66 = dust-tight + powerful water jet (gearless Sailfin control enclosure).

**ISR** — Inverter Spike Resistant. Refers to the motor winding wire used on Sailfin motors (200°C rated), which extends life when driven by a VFD.

**Load reactor** — an inductor on the VFD output that limits voltage spikes on long motor cables. Required on Lenze VFD runs of 250-400 ft (230 V), 160-250 ft (460 V), or 125-160 ft (600 V).

**LVC** — Low Voltage Control. Envira-North's 0-10 V analogue controller. Daisy-chains up to 7 fans (on ZoneAIR) or up to 10 Schneider VFDs, max 700-1000 ft of cable depending on VFD type.

**Modbus / ModBus RTU** — serial communication protocol. Used by ModAIR, Jazz Fan, Aisle-Air, and FlexAIR for HMI/BAS integration.

**NEMA4X** — enclosure rating meaning watertight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for outdoor or washdown environments. Schneider VFD, TouchAIR, and FlexAIR enclosures are NEMA4X.

**Nidec** — motor manufacturer used on the gearless Sailfin direct drive.

**Nord** — gearmotor manufacturer used on the geared Sailfin ("Helical in-line Nord gearmotor").

**OWSJ** — Open Web Steel Joist. One of the supported mount types for all Sailfin and Jazz fans.

**Purlin Z** — Z-shaped roof purlin. A supported mount type for Sailfin and Jazz fans.

**RJ45** — the modular connector on the end of a CAT-5 cable. The SmartAIR CAT-5 must land on the RJ45 port *inside* the Schneider VFD enclosure, not the external one on the drive.

**T1 / T2 / T3** — the three motor output terminals on a VFD.

**TFD-1** — Temperature controller add-on for the LVC. Uses a set point + modulation band + minimum-ventilation-off temperature to drive the fan speed.

**VFD** — Variable Frequency Drive. Every Altra-Air fan requires one VFD per fan. The VFD provides soft-start, variable-speed control, and over-temperature / overload protection (so motor thermal-protection wires in the junction box are not used).
