  - 19+ broken cross-reference slugs (systemic: generic family slugs don't resolve to files)

- **Contradicted claims (vs source):** 1 (blade alloy 6005-T61 vs 6063-T5)
- **Cross-wiki conflicts:** 6 (Lenze controls vs gearless compatibility is the most significant)

### Recommended actions (priority order)

1. **Fix broken source_doc filename** — Update all 9 files referencing `Envira-North 2025__Catalogue_EN__Rev-01 (pdfplumber).txt` to the actual filename `Envira-North 2025__Catalogue__EN__Rev-03__11.27.2025 (pdfplumber).txt`. This is the highest-risk issue: claims in warranty, maintenance, mounting, and 5 application guides currently cannot be verified.

2. **Resolve Lenze ↔ gearless compatibility conflict** — Verify with Envira-North whether EssentialAIR, CommandAIR, and ZoneAIR are compatible with gearless Sailfin fans. Update either the control files' `works_with` or the product files' `controls_compatible` accordingly.

3. **Fix TouchAIR `works_with`** — Add Sailfin products (or generic family slugs if convention is adopted) to TouchAIR's `works_with` list. Currently it only lists `[schneider-vfd, modair, jazz-fan]` despite being compatible with all Sailfin fans.

4. **Decide on generic family slugs** — Either (a) add a CLAUDE.md convention that `sailfin-geared` and `sailfin-gearless` are valid family-level slugs, or (b) expand all `works_with`/`applies_to`/`compares` to list every size. Recommend option (a) for maintainability.

5. **Resolve sailfin-gearless-8ft.md status** — Either add it to index.md or remove the file. Its existence contradicts the index note.

6. **Update image-descriptions frontmatter** — Add `id` and `last_verified` fields to all 19 image-descriptions files, and standardize `source_pdf` to `source_docs` format.

7. **Add `> **Unverified:**` callout** to the blade alloy discrepancy in `sailfin-geared-vs-gearless.md`.

8. **Verify gearless 8ft specs** — The 8ft gearless sharing identical specs (HP, power, RPM) with the 12ft gearless is unusual. Confirm against source docs.

9. **Standardize guide category values** — Align `category` fields with the template enum (`electrical`, `mounting`, `maintenance`, `warranty`).

10. **Add BMS to glossary** — It's used in wiki text but not defined.
