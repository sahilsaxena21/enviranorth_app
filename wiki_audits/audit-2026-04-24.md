### Top 10 Recommended Actions (priority order)

1. **Resolve Lenze vs gearless compatibility conflict** -- Verify with Envira-North whether EssentialAIR, CommandAIR, and ZoneAIR are compatible with gearless Sailfin fans. This is the highest-impact issue: 3 control files claim compatibility, but NO gearless product lists them. Additionally, some gearless product body text contradicts the frontmatter. Update either the control files' `works_with` or the product files' `controls_compatible` and body text accordingly.

2. **Fix the geared-8ft "no gearless exists" contradiction** -- `products/sailfin-geared-8ft.md` body text states "There is no gearless 8 FT Sailfin" but `products/sailfin-gearless-8ft.md` exists with full specs and is referenced by other files. Update the geared 8ft body text to acknowledge the gearless variant exists.

3. **Resolve sailfin-gearless-8ft.md index status** -- Either add it to index.md (removing the contradictory note) or remove the file. Its existence contradicts the index note "source documents do not include an 8 FT gearless Sailfin."

4. **Fix SmartAIR Multi-Fan `works_with`** -- Currently only lists gearless sizes. Add all geared size slugs to match the parent `smartair.md` which correctly lists both.

5. **Expand control `works_with` to explicit size slugs** -- Per CLAUDE_FULL.md convention, family slugs (`sailfin-geared`, `sailfin-gearless`) are NOT valid in `works_with` fields. Update EssentialAIR, CommandAIR, and ZoneAIR to list explicit size slugs (once the gearless compatibility question in action #1 is resolved).

6. **Resolve blade alloy discrepancy** -- The installation guide says 6005-T61 for both geared and gearless; the D&E guides say 6063-T5 for gearless. Some product files say "6005" and others say "6063". Add a `> **Unverified:**` callout to `comparisons/sailfin-geared-vs-gearless.md` and each affected product file until confirmed with Envira-North.

7. **Fix the "5.7 A" feature claim on gearless products** -- All gearless Sailfin files say "Maximum 5.7 A draw" in their Key Features section, but all spec tables show 5.0 A @ 230V. The 5.7 A figure appears to be the geared 24ft's draw. Correct to 5.0 A or verify against source.

8. **Standardize guide category values** -- Align `category` fields with the template enum. `electrical-install-lenze.md` and `electrical-install-schneider.md` should be `electrical`; `mounting-general.md` should be `mounting`; `warranty.md` should be `warranty`.

9. **Update image-descriptions frontmatter** -- Add `id` and `last_verified` fields to all 19 image-descriptions files, and standardize `source_pdf` to `source_docs` format.

10. **Add BMS and LinkAir to glossary** -- BMS is used in multiple wiki files but not defined. LinkAir is mentioned in `controls/touchair.md` as a BACnet option but has no glossary entry or wiki page.
